Why Mexico

The Premier Nearshoring Destination

Mexico offers a powerful combination of geographic proximity, trade advantages, and a rapidly growing industrial talent pool that no other market can match.

  • ✓
    USMCA Trade Framework

    Duty-free access to US & Canadian markets with simplified rules of origin for manufactured goods.

  • ✓
    IMMEX Manufacturing Program

    Import raw materials and equipment duty-free for the manufacture of exported products.

  • ✓
    Growing Talent Pool

    120K+ engineering graduates annually with strong technical expertise in automation and manufacturing.

  • ✓
    Cost Competitiveness

    Competitive labor costs with significantly lower logistics overhead versus Asian manufacturing alternatives.
