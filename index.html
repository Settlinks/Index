The Opportunity

Why Mexico, Why Now

Mexico has become North America's premier nearshoring destination for industrial automation — driven by USMCA trade advantages, a growing engineering talent pool, and proximity to the US market.

USMCA Preferential Access

Zero-tariff manufacturing exports to both the US and Canada — unmatched by any other nearshoring destination worldwide.

Industry 4.0 Growth Hub

Mexico's automotive and aerospace sectors are rapidly adopting robotics, cobots, IoT, and AI-driven manufacturing systems.

Engineering Talent at Scale

130,000+ STEM graduates annually in Monterrey, Querétaro, and Guadalajara — Mexico's technology triangle.
