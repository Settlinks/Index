Our Story

The Bridge Between
Innovation & Opportunity

Founded in 2024, Settlinks is a holdings company dedicated to making Mexico's industrial automation opportunity accessible to the world's best manufacturers.

Our Mission

Connecting Global Innovation to Mexico's Manufacturing Ecosystem

We bridge foreign manufacturers in industrial automation with Mexico's booming market, creating shared value through strategic partnerships, capital deployment, and sustainable long-term expansion.

Our Vision

The Premier Gateway for Industrial Automation in Mexico

To become the definitive partner for foreign firms entering Mexico's manufacturing sector — driving Industry 4.0 adoption, economic growth, and technological advancement across the region.

Who We Are

A Holdings Company Built for Expansion

Settlinks operates at the intersection of industrial manufacturing, cross-border legal expertise, and venture capital. We don't just advise — we invest alongside you, taking equity positions and acting as committed partners in your Mexican venture.

Our founders bring over 20 years of combined experience in nearshoring, Mexico's regulatory environment, and industrial automation deployment across North America and beyond.
