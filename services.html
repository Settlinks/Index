What We Offer

End-to-End Services for Industrial Automation Expansion in Mexico

From first market analysis to full operational scale — eight integrated services designed around industrial automation manufacturers entering Mexico.

01

Market Entry Advisory

Strategic planning for entering Mexico's manufacturing hubs. Includes full market analysis, competitor benchmarking, target-sector mapping, and nearshoring strategy leveraging USMCA advantages. We identify which industrial corridors — Monterrey, Querétaro, Guadalajara, or Tijuana — best fit your product and client base.

USMCA StrategyMarket AnalysisCompetitor Mapping
02

Legal & Regulatory Compliance

Full assistance with IMMEX certification, import/export permits, business licenses, and compliance with Mexican labor, environmental, and trade regulations. We act as your official legal representative throughout the process, removing the risk of costly regulatory mistakes during market entry.

IMMEX CertificationSAT RegistrationLabor LawEnvironmental
03

Site Selection & Facility Setup

Identifying optimal locations within certified industrial parks across Monterrey, Querétaro, San Luis Potosí, and Saltillo. We evaluate infrastructure, utilities, proximity to customers, and workforce availability. Then we manage facility design, build-out, and setup for your specific automation lines — including robotics cells and IoT infrastructure.

Industrial ParksFacility DesignIoT Infrastructure
04

Supply Chain & Logistics Optimization

Building resilient regional supply chains from the ground up. We map local suppliers, negotiate freight and customs partnerships, and design logistics flows that minimize cost and lead times. Special expertise in bonded warehouse structures and cross-border customs coordination under IMMEX.

Supplier MappingCustoms StrategyBonded Warehouse
05

Venture Capital & Equity Partnerships

Milestone-based funding structured for industrial automation firms entering Mexico. We invest as shareholders or venture capitalists, taking equity stakes in joint ventures. Investment tranches are tied to real operational outcomes: IMMEX approval, facility commissioning, first customer delivery, and production scale targets. We only invest where we believe in the opportunity.

Equity StakesJoint VenturesMilestone Funding
06

Talent Recruitment & Training

Sourcing certified engineers, automation technicians, and operations staff for industrial facilities across Mexico. We tap university networks in Monterrey's Tecnológico, UANL, and ITESM campuses. Training programs cover PLCs (Siemens, Allen-Bradley), SCADA systems, collaborative robot operation, and quality systems aligned to your standards.

Engineer RecruitmentPLC TrainingSCADACobot Certification
07

Distribution Channel Development

Establishing and managing sales and distribution networks in Mexico. We identify and vet local distributors, VAR partners, and system integrators aligned with your product category. Channel agreements are structured to protect your IP and pricing, with governance frameworks ensuring quality and brand consistency across the network.

Distributor NetworkVAR PartnersChannel Agreements
08

Automation Integration Services

Expert technical support for implementing Industry 4.0 solutions within Mexican facilities. From collaborative robot deployment to predictive maintenance AI and industrial IoT sensor networks — our engineers manage integration, commissioning, and staff training. We bridge the technology gap between your equipment and Mexico's operational environment.

Industry 4.0Cobot DeploymentPredictive MaintenanceIIoT
