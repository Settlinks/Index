Our Story

The Bridge Between Global Innovation and Mexico's Industrial Future

Founded in 2024, Settlinks was built on a simple conviction: foreign automation manufacturers deserve a partner who is invested — literally — in their success in Mexico.

Who We Are

A Holdings Company Built on Partnership

Settlinks is a holdings company specializing in partnering with foreign manufacturers in the industrial automation sector to expand into Mexico's thriving market. Unlike traditional consultancies, we enter every engagement as a stakeholder — as shareholders, venture capitalists, or legal representatives — creating full alignment between our goals and yours.

We operate from two offices: Toronto, Canada (where many of our international clients are based) and Monterrey, Mexico (the heart of Mexico's industrial automation belt). This dual presence gives us a unique perspective on both sides of the market.

🎯 Our Mission

To bridge global innovation in industrial automation with Mexico's manufacturing ecosystem, creating sustainable value through strategic partnerships and equity-aligned growth.

🔭 Our Vision

To become the premier gateway for foreign automation firms entering Mexico, driving Industry 4.0 adoption and measurable economic growth across North America.

⚖️ Our Commitment

We only take on clients where we genuinely believe in the opportunity. When we invest our capital and reputation, we ensure every engagement is built to succeed.

Our Journey

Built for This Moment in Manufacturing

The nearshoring wave didn't happen overnight. We spent years building the network, expertise, and legal infrastructure to be ready when the opportunity arrived.

2021

The Idea Takes Shape

Founders identify a clear gap: foreign automation manufacturers want Mexico access but lack trustworthy, invested partners on the ground.

2022

Network Built in Monterrey

Two years of relationship-building with industrial park operators, IMMEX specialists, and tier-1 automotive suppliers across NL and QRO.

2023

Legal & VC Structure Formed

2024

First Partners Onboarded
