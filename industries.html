Our Focus

Industrial Automation — Every Niche, Every Layer

We specialize exclusively in industrial automation. From robotics and cobots to PLCs, sensors, and AI-driven maintenance — we know the technology and the market.

Technology Niches

The Automation Segments We Serve

Every niche in industrial automation has different buyers, regulatory considerations, and integration requirements in Mexico. Our expertise spans the full stack.

🤖

Robotics & Cobots

From traditional industrial robots to the latest collaborative robot platforms. Mexico's automotive sector is one of the fastest-growing cobot markets globally.

  • Assembly line integration
  • Pick-and-place systems
  • Welding & painting automation
  • Collaborative human-robot workstations
  • Safety certification support (CE, UL)
📡

IoT & Smart Sensors

Industrial IoT hardware and sensor manufacturers benefit enormously from Mexico's electronics manufacturing base and proximity to US design centers.

  • Vibration & temperature sensors
  • Machine vision systems
  • Industrial gateway devices
  • RFID and asset tracking
  • Edge computing hardware
🖥️

PLCs & SCADA Systems

Control system manufacturers face a large and underserved replacement cycle across Mexico's aging manufacturing infrastructure — a significant distribution opportunity.

  • PLC hardware & software
  • HMI panels & interfaces
  • SCADA platforms
  • Industrial networking (EtherNet/IP, Profinet)
  • Motion control systems
🔧

Predictive Maintenance AI

AI-driven maintenance platforms are finding rapid adoption in Mexico's automotive plants, where unplanned downtime costs are extremely high.

  • Condition monitoring software
  • AI anomaly detection platforms
  • OEE optimization systems
  • Maintenance workflow tools
  • Digital twin solutions
⚡

Power Electronics & Drives

Variable frequency drives, servo systems, and power management equipment have a deep installed base opportunity across Mexico's food, pharma, and automotive sectors.

  • Variable frequency drives (VFDs)
  • Servo drives & motors
  • Power quality equipment
  • UPS & backup systems
  • Energy management platforms
🏭

Turnkey System Integrators

For manufacturers who provide complete automated production lines or turnkey systems — Mexico's expanding industrial parks offer large greenfield opportunities.

  • Conveyor & material handling
  • Automated inspection lines
  • End-of-line packaging systems
  • Clean room automation
  • Pharma & food-grade systems
End-User Markets

Where Your Technology Gets Deployed

Mexico's industrial automation demand is concentrated in four high-growth manufacturing sectors — all undergoing rapid modernization.

🚗

Automotive

Mexico is the 7th largest vehicle producer globally. Tier-1 and Tier-2 suppliers concentrated in Monterrey, Saltillo, San Luis Potosí, and Aguascalientes are undergoing intensive automation upgrades to meet EV production demands.

Largest automation buyer in Mexico
✈️

Aerospace

Mexico's aerospace cluster — particularly in Querétaro, Baja California, and Sonora — is expanding rapidly. AS9100-compliant automation for precision machining, composites, and avionics assembly is in high demand.

400+ aerospace companies in MX
📱

Electronics

Consumer electronics, EV batteries, and industrial electronics manufacturing is growing in Tijuana, Juárez, and Monterrey. SMT automation, vision inspection, and test & measurement equipment have strong demand.

2nd largest electronics exporter to USA
💊

Pharmaceutical

Mexico's pharma sector — centered in CDMX and Jalisco — is modernizing production lines to meet FDA and COFEPRIS standards. Serialization, clean room robotics, and automated inspection systems are priority investments.

$15B+ pharma market
🥫

Food & Beverage

One of Mexico's largest manufacturing sectors, with growing demand for automated packaging, filling, and palletizing solutions. FSMA and NOM-251 compliance drives automation investment in medium and large producers.

$120B+ food processing sector
⚙️

Metal & Heavy Industry

Steel, aluminum, and heavy fabrication facilities across Coahuila, Nuevo León, and Tamaulipas are investing in automated welding, CNC, and quality inspection systems to serve the automotive and construction markets.

Major US export supplier

🏭
Querétaro, QRO.

Aerospace and advanced manufacturing. Fastest-growing industrial state in Mexico.

🚢
Tijuana & Juárez

Border maquiladora zones. Electronics, medical devices, and consumer goods.

🔋
Saltillo & San Luis Potosí

Automotive OEM clusters. BMW, GM, Stellantis, and Audi supplier networks.
